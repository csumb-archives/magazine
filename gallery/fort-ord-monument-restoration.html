Fort Ord Monument Restoration

  • University News
  • Alumni News
  • Student Profiles
  • Archive
  • Digital Edition

The Watershed Institute's Return of the Natives program has a long history of getting students involved in land restoration. It's the perfect preparation for employer Burleson Consulting of Sacramento, who has hired several alumni to lead the restoration they've been contracted to do at the Fort Ord National Monument. 

This photo gallery shows how CSUMB alums now employed at Burleson have partnered with the Watershed Institute to form restoration crews that are transforming Fort Ord from blighted land to National Monument. You can read the feature story here. All photos by Steve Zmak.
