Posted on May 13, 2013

Cal State Monterey Bay mascot Monte Rey just got a new look and has been flaunting it all over campus. He loves showing off his moves at games and special events with the help of the Otter Dance Team and Blue Crue. His favorite dance? Gangnam Style, of course.

Monte’s also active on Facebook and Twitter, where he especially loves celebrating with newly admitted CSUMB students and posting about clams and other “otter nonsense.”

Connect with him at facebook.com/CSUMBMonteRey or@CSUMBMonteRey on Twitter.

– Liz MacDonald
