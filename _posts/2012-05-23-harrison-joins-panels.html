Posted on May 23, 2012

CSU Monterey Bay President Dianne Harrison in July was named to the Executive Board of California Campus Compact, a coalition of colleges and universities committed to civic engagement. The compact’s mission is building the capacity of colleges and universities to advance civic and community engagement throughout the state.

Also, in May, Gov. Jerry Brown appointed Dr. Harrison to serve on the Western Interstate Commission for Higher Education (WICHE) through October 2014. WICHE’s mission is to expand access to higher education for residents of the Western states.
