Posted on May 23, 2012

Dear alumni and friends,

The 2011-2012 academic year is already well under way, and this has been an exciting fall at CSUMB, with many campus improvements, the launch of four new bachelor’s degrees and a record fall enrollment of more than 5,000 students. 

The Alumni Association wants you to be part of the unfolding story of your university, a place we are proud to call our alma mater. On July 1, by a vote of the alumni board, we became a non-dues membership program. Association benefits are now open to all degree-holding alumni of CSUMB. We hope this will encourage you to support student scholarships, as well as sharing your voice and your time.

As president, I look forward to meeting as many of you as possible at special events, including the Have a Heart for Students fund-raising dinner and auction on Feb. 25. On behalf of our entire board and all of our current and future students, thank you for staying connected to CSUMB and continuing the Vision!

Sincerely,
Melody Rico
Class of 2006
Alumni Association President
