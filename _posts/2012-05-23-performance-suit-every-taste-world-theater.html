Posted on May 23, 2012

This year’s lineup at the World Theater once again offers a wide variety of entertainment options. The season started with the National Acrobats of the People’s Republic of China. On Oct. 20, the Alley Cats brought their own contemporary style to tunes from the 1950s and ’60s.

The December People will return by popular demand, bringing a contemporary sound to holiday music. The Nov. 12 concert will benefit local food banks and the SPCA, as concert-goers will be encouraged to bring canned food for people or pets. The spring lineup will be available atCSUMB.EDU/worldtheater.

The December People perform Nov. 13, 2010, to a sellout crowd in the World Theater. They return to CSUMB Nov. 12.
