Posted on May 09, 2012

Photo caption above: CSU Monterey Bay senior Adán Romero in a classroom with biology professors Aparna Sreenivasan (left) and Henrik Kibak.

CSU Monterey Bay senior Adán Romero of Salinas will head to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology this fall to begin work on a Ph.D. in biological engineering. One of 810 seniors who will graduate May 22, Adán is going to MIT on a full scholarship thanks to the support of outstanding faculty mentors and financial help that made his journey possible.

Support from community members is the key to student opportunity.
