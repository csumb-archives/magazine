New chancellor leads CSU system

Dr. Timothy White took over as chancellor of the California State University system in late December, replacing Charles Reed, who retired after 14 years.

White had been chancellor of the University of California at Riverside. Earlier in his career, he served as provost at Oregon State University and then president of the University of Idaho. He earned degrees from Diablo Community College, Fresno State University, Cal State Hayward (now East Bay) and UC Berkeley.
