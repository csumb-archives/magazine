CSUMB Magazine Fall 2011

University prepares students for a profession in demand

The School of Information Technology & Communication Design prepares students for such high demand fields as software engineering and web design.

 

Uncharted waters

Seafloor mapping researcher Dr. Rikk Kvitek has used grant money to develop a new research vessel capable of gathering data in near-shore areas off limits to larger craft.

 

Fostering success

CSUMB is nationally recognized for its efforts to reach and assist migrant students who otherwise might not have the opportunity to attend and graduate college.

 

Paying it forward

Mike and Linda Dorn's support for the Pay It Forward scholarship program is enshrined in a new name for the library atruim.

A boon for small business

The Small Business Development Center provides management and technical assistance to small businesses and entrepreneurs along the Highway 101 corridor from King City to Gilroy.

A will to win

The men's golf team defeated Lynn University of Boca Raton, Fla. to win the 2011 NCAA National Championship on May 20. This marks the university's first ever national championship victory -- and a major step forward for Otter Athletics.
